Abstract

The world is a beautiful place for those who can see it. Show us the world you see through your camera lens. Capture moments of ‘hope’ or the times when the beauty of ‘opposites’ strike your heart. Submit your individual entries based on the themes mentioned above and stand a chance to win amazing prizes. Remember to be not offensive and inappropriate. Happy capturing!

Rules and Regulations

  • This is a group event. Each team must have 2-3 members.
  • A maximum of 2 teams is allowed from each institute.
  • Each team is supposed to make a story using a maximum of 6 photographs. There should be a write-up of not more than 500 words describing the story.
  • Entries with offensive or inappropriate content will be disqualified.
  • The entries must be submitted on or before 30th November 2019. Any entry sent later will not be accepted.
  • The submission of entry would be regarded as the contestants having given permission to IICM 2019 to use the photographs for publicity and other purposes in the form of broadcast, electronic media, print or social networking without any remuneration.
  • The submitted images should be in JPEG format (150 dpi recommended).
  • Digital enhancements that distort the reality of the image are not allowed, only basic editing like sharpening, contrast adjustment, colour correction, and simple cropping is allowed.
  • Both color and monochrome images will be accepted.
  • The entries should not have any watermark on them.
  • The judge’s decision will be final and binding.
  • The rules of the competition will not be altered or relaxed for any contestant regardless of the situation.
  • If it comes to notice of event coordinators that the participant has violated any rules mentioned above, the entry will be disqualified as per their discretion.
  • Entries from each institute should be uploaded to a Google Drive/OneDrive/Dropbox folder and shared with iicm2019@iisertvm.ac.in.
