About IICM
The Inter IISER Cultural Meet(IICM) is the annual cultural fest-cum-competition amongst the leading research institutes across the country. In this grand event, students from seven different IISERs (Thiruvananthapuram, Pune, Mohali, Bhopal, Tirupati, Berhampur and Kolkata),CEBS Mumbai and IISc Bangalore will represent their respective institutes in several competitions of art, music, dance, theatre, filmmaking, literature, and photography spread over a span of three days. This year, IICM will be organised by IISER Thiruvananthapuram in the lush greens of its main campus at Vithura, Kerala. With huge participation and turnout of students for the intercollegiate events, IICM‘18 did set the bars high, and we aim to cross our limits as always, and make IICM‘19 even greater.
